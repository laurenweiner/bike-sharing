# bike-sharing

[link to dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/shared/BQY2PMRKQ?:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)


## Overview

The purpose of this analysis is to understand when, where, and by who the bikes are used most often.

## Results

Usage in the first hour is steady for 0-60 minutes. Then, trips become longer and longer within the 1-2 hour blocks.
<img width="646" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302196-0caf364e-ca89-4498-af71-0dc63471eb92.png">


Men are the predominant user, especially for longer trips.
<img width="725" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302245-2acadde0-9e31-473b-b3e4-345657b25313.png">


Primetime is during commuting hours (8am and 6pm). Usage is steady on the weekends.
<img width="756" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302257-c692f694-b232-4b14-8312-5095d6fa5bb2.png">


Female usage is much lighter than male usage. Men use the bikes consistently during commuting hours.
<img width="762" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302288-563a4d07-669e-4ab3-9c9b-a6e062bd0368.png">


Women use bikes more on weekends. Men use bikes more on weekdays.
<img width="830" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302303-213aba60-71a6-42e3-952b-1a5ec7814ec4.png">


Peak hours are around 8pm and 6pm.
<img width="806" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302311-b8a1935b-15fd-44a1-8593-53f311324ac3.png">


Bikes are used most in lower Manhattan.
<img width="765" alt="image" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/92737670/160302324-a3026c8b-86f7-4bdf-a7b9-a9de289ba807.png">


## Summary

In general, men are the most predominant users. They use the bikes (probably) for commuting to and from work, and over the weekends.
